Our Story & Vibe

A Hillside Escape
Above the City Noise

Perched along the scenic Sumulong Highway in Antipolo, Sumu Hillside Cafe was born for those who crave good food alongside an unforgettable backdrop — the glittering skyline of Metro Manila laid out beneath you.

Our signature is simplicity and generosity: every dish is crafted to be shared, every serving made to impress. Whether you settle into one of our cozy indoor booths or take a seat on the breezy step-free outdoor terrace, you're guaranteed a moment worth remembering.

  • ✦ Indoor Booth Seating — intimate, aesthetic, and air-conditioned
  • ✦ Step-free Outdoor Terrace — wheelchair accessible, perfect for families and dates
  • ✦ Generous Portions — every dish is designed to be shared between two
  • ✦ Value for Money — premium hillside dining at ₱1–500 per person
