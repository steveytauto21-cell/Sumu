The Sumu Experience

More Than Just a Cafe.
It's a Hillside Moment.

Nestled along Sumulong Highway in Antipolo, Sumu Cafe is your hillside escape from the city's noise. Whether you're settling into a cozy indoor booth or breathing in the breeze on our step-free outdoor terrace, every seat comes with one of the best views of Metro Manila's skyline.

Every dish at Sumu is crafted for sharing — generous portions designed for two, paired with flavors that make you stay longer than you planned. Perfect for dates, family afternoons, or a slow Sunday coffee.
